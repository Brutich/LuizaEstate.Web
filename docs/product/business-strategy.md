# Business Strategy

## Mission
Build a highly profitable boutique real estate business around Luiza's expertise.

## North Star
Create a business that consistently generates high-quality clients through reputation, content and digital channels.

## 1-year goals
- Relaunch website
- Build measurable lead funnel
- Collect testimonials and case studies

## 3-year goals
- Strong personal brand
- Stable inbound lead flow
- High-margin service portfolio

## 5-year vision
A scalable boutique real estate brand with processes, automation and the ability to expand beyond a single specialist.

## KPIs
- Qualified leads
- Conversion to consultation
- Conversion to transaction
- Revenue
- Profit margin
- Repeat and referral clients
